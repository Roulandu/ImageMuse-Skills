# Failure Repair Recipes

Use this reference for Image2 adult glamour and fashion prompt debugging.

## Rejected Prompt

Likely causes: explicit wording, minor-coded sexualization, real-person sexualization, body-part fixation, coercive framing, private/voyeuristic setting, risky element stacking.

Repair:
- Remove red terms.
- Make subject original and mid-20s or older.
- Replace sexual intent with editorial/fashion/commercial intent.
- Replace body close-ups with whole-body silhouette or garment-fit language.
- Replace private access with studio, lookbook, lifestyle, or catalog context.

## Too Conservative

Do not add "more sexy". Add:
- Stronger fashion styling.
- Better garment fit and material detail.
- More intentional posture and silhouette.
- Richer lighting and color contrast.
- Editorial camera direction.
- Clearer mature confidence and face expression.

## Face Looks Too Young

Add:
- mature adult face structure, mid-20s or older.
- non-childlike proportions, adult jaw/cheek structure.
- composed expression, mature styling, refined makeup.
- avoid school/student/cute-childlike cues.

## Too Suggestive

Repair:
- Return the image to commercial portrait, fashion editorial, or product lookbook.
- Use natural standing/seated posture.
- Use eye-level or slight high editorial camera.
- Broaden frame to include face, garment, posture, and setting.
- Remove slipping garments, low-angle gaze, wet/bedroom/intimate cues, and body-part focal language.

## Body Or Hands Malformed

Add:
- realistic anatomy, stable posture, natural limb placement.
- correct hand structure and finger count.
- natural shoulder, waist, hip, and leg proportions.
- avoid extreme twisting, extreme close crop, and impossible pose.

## Not High-End Enough

Add:
- cohesive palette, intentional negative space, controlled background.
- material details, seams, fabric texture, and garment structure.
- soft directional lighting, subtle shadows, realistic skin texture.
- editorial or commercial purpose.
